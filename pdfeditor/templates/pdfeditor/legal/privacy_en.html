🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

PDF Platform (pdf.micutu.com) is an open-source project operated from the European Union by its developer. For any question about your data, write to 984.alexmihai@gmail.com.

What we process

  • Account data — username, e-mail address (for confirmation and password resets) and your password (stored only as a hash). If you enable two-factor authentication or passkeys, we also store the technical data they require.
  • Your documents — the PDFs/images you upload and the outputs generated from them. They are processed exclusively for the operations you request.
  • Usage and security metadata — an audit log of operations (user, IP address, browser, operation type, file names, timestamps), active sessions, and failed login attempts.
  • Aggregate statistics — we use a self-hosted, cookie-less analytics service that builds no individual profiles.

How long we keep it

  • Files (uploaded and generated) are deleted automatically after at most 24 hours.
  • Backups (database and files) are rotated after 14 days.
  • Your account exists until you delete it — deletion is self-service from your profile page and removes your files, history, API keys and sessions.
  • The audit log is retained for as long as the account exists, for security purposes.

Where data goes

  • Cloudflare — traffic to the site passes through Cloudflare's network (DNS, proxy, DDoS protection).
  • Cloudflare R2 — processing outputs may be temporarily mirrored to R2 object storage for reliable downloads; they are purged together with the files, within the same 24-hour window.
  • Groq (US) — only when you use the AI features (chat with PDF, summarize, rephrase), relevant text excerpts from the document are sent to the Groq API, subject to Groq's privacy policy. If you don't use the AI features, your documents never leave our server.
  • E-mail — confirmation/reset messages are sent through our own self-hosted mail server.

We do not sell or rent your data. There are no ads and no advertising trackers.

Cookies and local storage

  • sessionid — keeps you signed in;
  • csrftoken — protection against request forgery;
  • django_language — the language you picked;
  • your theme preference (dark/light) lives in localStorage, not in a cookie.

All strictly functional — no tracking or advertising cookies.

Legal basis (GDPR)

We process data to perform the contract (providing the service you request), on legitimate interest (security, abuse prevention) and, for the AI features, on your explicit action of invoking them.

Your rights

  • Access and portability — you can export all your account data from the profile page.
  • Erasure — you can delete your account entirely, at any time, from the profile page.
  • Rectification — you can change your e-mail address from the profile.
  • Complaint — if something seems wrong, write to us first; you always have the right to contact your supervisory authority (in Romania: ANSPDCP).

Security

Connections are HTTPS-only, passwords and API keys are stored only as hashes, uploaded files are scanned for malware, and your account can be further protected with two-factor authentication (TOTP) or passkeys. Operations are rate-limited and audit-logged.

Children

The service is not directed at children under 16.

Changes

If this policy changes, we update this page and the date above. Material changes will be announced visibly on the site.
